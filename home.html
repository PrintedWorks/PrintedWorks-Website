Our Mission

Printed Works is a student run club that is co-founded and sponsored by the entrepreneurial business Spyder3D and is focused on allowing students to gain real world business experience. In Printed Works, students design and engineer products that are commissioned by other school clubs or outside businesses, This is a great opportunity for students to learn how to use industry standard machines such as large format printers, laser engravers, 3-D printers, and garment production technology. Not only will students learn how to use machines, but they will also learn communication skills, leadership, and business management. This club is the perfect way for students to gain real-world experience in the design and engineering field.
