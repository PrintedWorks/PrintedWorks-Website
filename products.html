What Do We Make?

Direct-To-Garment

Direct-To-Garment Shirt

DTG is a process in which a design is directly printed onto a garment, most commonly used for high quality and easily producible shirt designs. DTG is known for the natural feel and picture quality it produces. Inkjet technology is used to print the design onto the garment. White ink is printed first, and then color ink is printed on top. The ink used is water-based and eco-friendly. DTG printing is very precise, so your design will look exactly how you want it to on the shirt.

Banner

Banner

Banners are printed on a large format Roland printer. Banners are made of a polyester fabric that is tear resistant and durable. They are printed with a high resolution to ensure that your design looks great. Banners are perfect for indoor or outdoor use and can be hung using grommets. Banners start at $2/sqft.

Stickers

Stickers

Stickers are printed on a large format Roland printer. This machine is able to print large stickers with high quality and resolution. Made of durable adhesive fabric that is repositionable and removable.

Engravings

Laser Engravings

Laser engravings are a type of product decoration that uses a laser to etch a design into a product. This can be done on a variety of materials, including wood, glass, metal, and acrylic. Laser engravings are permanent and will not fade or wear over time. They are also very precise, so you can create intricate designs.

Heat Transfer Vinyl

Heat Transfer Vinyl

Heat transfer vinyl is a type of vinyl that can be used with an iron or heat press to create designs, logos, and text on fabric. Heat transfer vinyl comes in a wide variety of colors, including metallics, glitter, and fluorescent. It can be used on a variety of fabrics, including cotton, polyester, and leather.

Embroidery

Embroidery

Embroidery is a process where thread is stitched into fabric to create a design. Embroidery is most commonly used for company logos, but can be used for any type of design. Embroidery is very durable, so it is perfect for designs that will be worn often or for designs that need to last a long time. The price for embroidery varies on many factors, to receive a quote collect information on size, quantity, garment type, and artwork.

Window Perforation Film

Window Perforation Film

Perforation Window Film is a type of window film that is printed with a design and then has tiny holes punched out of it. The holes are small enough that they are not visible from a distance, but they allow light to pass through. Perforations allow you to see out but not in, making it ideal for security and promotional purposes

Skateboards

Skateboards

Grip tape is a sheet of sandpaper that is applied to the top of a skateboard deck. It is used to provide traction for the skater's feet and to customize the look of the skateboard. Grip tape comes in a variety of colors, allowing for intricate and detailed designs.
